Objective: Enhance patient experience through intuitive design

Key Benefits

For the Patient

  • Glanceability: Patients can determine the state of their healthcare in under 1 second of looking at their lock screen.
  • Reduced Anxiety: Clearly defined symbols remove the ambiguity of "System Update" messages.

For the Pharmacy

  • Call Volume Reduction: By proactively using ‚ö†Ô∏è and üõë, we stop patients from calling to ask about delays.
  • Efficiency: Streamlined communication allows technicians to focus on filling rather than phone triage.

Risk Mitigation & Implementation

  • Double Encoding: Every notification includes both the symbol and the written status (e.g., "‚úÖ READY") to ensure those with color-blindness or older devices can still interpret the message.
  • Tone Consistency: The system uses professional, concise language to balance the casual nature of emojis.
  • Character Limits: Notifications are optimized to stay under 160 characters (GSM segments) to minimize SMS overhead costs.
